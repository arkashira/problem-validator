# Marketing Plan
## Positioning
Problem Validator: "Systematically validate your startup idea and measure willingness-to-pay to ensure a successful launch."
Alternatives:
1. "De-risk your entrepreneurial journey with data-driven idea validation."
2. "Transform your idea into a viable business with our problem validation toolkit."
3. "Unlock the potential of your startup by identifying real problems and willing customers."

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
1. **Alex Chen**: Founder of a pre-seed startup, daily job involves product development and market research, budget: $5,000/month for marketing and validation tools.
2. **Maya Patel**: Entrepreneur with a validated idea, daily job focuses on refining the product and preparing for launch, budget: $10,000/month for growth hacking and customer acquisition.
3. **Ethan Kim**: CEO of an early-stage startup, daily job involves strategic planning and investor relations, budget: $20,000/month for innovation and competitive analysis.

## Channels
1. **LinkedIn Ads**: Targeted ads for entrepreneurs and startup founders, estimated CAC: $50/user.
2. **Content Marketing (Blog)**: SEO-optimized articles and guides on idea validation and startup success, estimated CAC: $20/user.
3. **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaborations with popular startup influencers and thought leaders, estimated CAC: $100/user.

## Content Cadence
Week 1-2: "Introduction to Problem Validation" and "10 Common Mistakes in Idea Validation" blog posts.
Week 3-4: "Measuring Willingness-to-Pay" and "Case Study: Successful Startup Validation" blog posts.
Week 5-6: "Problem Validation Toolkit" and "Expert Interview: Startup Success Stories" blog posts.
Week 7-12: Continue publishing bi-weekly blog posts, focusing on customer success stories, product updates, and industry trends.

## Launch Milestones
* D-30: Finalize marketing plan, launch blog, and start content creation.
* D-0: Launch Problem Validator product, announce on social media and LinkedIn.
* D+30: Analyze user feedback, iterate on the product, and optimize marketing channels.
* D+90: Review success metrics, plan for future development, and explore new marketing channels.

## Success Metrics
* DAU (Daily Active Users): 1,000 users by D+90.
* $MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) target: $100,000 by D+90.
