# User Stories for Problem Validator

## Epic 1: Idea Submission and Problem Identification
1. **User Story 1**
   - **As a** startup founder,
   - **I want** to submit my business idea,
   - **so that** I can receive feedback on potential problems it addresses.
   - **Acceptance Criteria:**
     - The submission form captures the business idea and target audience.
     - Users receive an acknowledgment of submission.
     - Feedback is provided within 48 hours.
   - **Estimated Complexity:** M

2. **User Story 2**
   - **As a** entrepreneur,
   - **I want** to receive a report on common problems in my industry,
   - **so that** I can refine my idea based on real market needs.
   - **Acceptance Criteria:**
     - The report includes at least 5 common problems in the specified industry.
     - The report is generated within 24 hours of idea submission.
     - Users can download the report in PDF format.
   - **Estimated Complexity:** M

3. **User Story 3**
   - **As a** product manager,
   - **I want** to analyze trends in problem submissions,
   - **so that** I can identify emerging market needs.
   - **Acceptance Criteria:**
     - A dashboard displays trends over time.
     - Users can filter trends by industry or problem type.
     - Data is updated in real-time.
   - **Estimated Complexity:** L

## Epic 2: Willingness-to-Pay Assessment
4. **User Story 4**
   - **As a** startup founder,
   - **I want** to conduct surveys to assess willingness-to-pay,
   - **so that** I can gauge market interest in my solution.
   - **Acceptance Criteria:**
     - Surveys can be customized with specific questions.
     - Users can share surveys via email or social media.
     - Results are compiled and presented in a summary report.
   - **Estimated Complexity:** M

5. **User Story 5**
   - **As a** business analyst,
   - **I want** to view aggregated willingness-to-pay data,
   - **so that** I can make informed decisions on pricing strategies.
   - **Acceptance Criteria:**
     - Data is displayed in charts and graphs.
     - Users can filter results by demographics.
     - Historical data is available for comparison.
   - **Estimated Complexity:** L

## Epic 3: Feedback and Iteration
6. **User Story 6**
   - **As a** startup founder,
   - **I want** to receive feedback on my refined idea,
   - **so that** I can iterate and improve my business model.
   - **Acceptance Criteria:**
     - Feedback is provided by industry experts.
     - Users can request specific areas of focus for feedback.
     - Feedback is delivered within 72 hours of request.
   - **Estimated Complexity:** M

7. **User Story 7**
   - **As a** product manager,
   - **I want** to track changes made to my idea based on feedback,
   - **so that** I can measure progress over time.
   - **Acceptance Criteria:**
     - A version history is maintained for each idea submission.
     - Users can view comments and suggestions from previous feedback.
     - Users can revert to a previous version if needed.
   - **Estimated Complexity:** L

## Epic 4: Community Engagement
8. **User Story 8**
   - **As a** startup founder,
   - **I want** to connect with other entrepreneurs,
   - **so that** I can share experiences and learn from each other.
   - **Acceptance Criteria:**
     - A community forum is available for discussions.
     - Users can create and join groups based on interests.
     - Notifications are sent for new posts and replies.
   - **Estimated Complexity:** M

9. **User Story 9**
   - **As a** user,
   - **I want** to rate and review the feedback I receive,
   - **so that** I can help improve the quality of the service.
   - **Acceptance Criteria:**
     - Users can submit ratings from 1 to 5 stars.
     - Reviews can be edited or deleted by the user.
     - Average ratings are displayed on the feedback page.
   - **Estimated Complexity:** S
